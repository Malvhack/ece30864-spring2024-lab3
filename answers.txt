Question C: The 3 Meetings are the Sprint Planning Meeting, which start the spring and determine what to complete in the upcoming sprint. Another is the Daily Scrum Meeting which is a daily meeting to plan for the day. The final is the Sprint Retrospective meeting which happens at the end of the sprint and discusses completed work.
